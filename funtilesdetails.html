My Project

Fun Tiles

For those who wish to play the piano but cannot afford to acquire one, Fun Tiles was developed. Because this programme included a beginner's module, it was beneficial and simple for a novice to utilise. All that was used to construct this fantastic programme was HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With this piano, the user can utilise the keyboard keys or click on the keys to perform a variety of tunes. On the piano, users can also change the volume and reveal or conceal the shortcut keys. Please click demo to try it out. Enjoy yourselves!
